PROJECT TITLE: The Tower
PURPOSE OF PROJECT: A system to allocate students to rooms/apartments and keep track of rental payments. 
SPEC DETAILS: A given apartment can house either all males, or all females. An unspecified gender will be allocated the entire apartment. Bedrooms are allocated on a first come first served basis (subject to gender conditions). The system will also be able to show all students and their rental charges based on the size of the apartment they are residing in.
